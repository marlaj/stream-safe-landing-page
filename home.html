Frequently Asked Questions

What is stream safe?

Stream safe is your gateway to safe video sharing. It is a video sharing platform that allows users to share video in a safe way that guarantees the rights of both the video owner and the viewer.

How do I know my content is safe?

Everything is encrypted using military grade 256-bit AES end-to-end encryption. Every video you share never leaves your device un-encrypted. It is always encrypted in a way that guarantees that only the intended receiver is able to view the video. Even our servers don't have access to your video

How is the encryption key shared?

The encryption keys are shared using the concept of PGP. This means that each user has a private and public key pair to ensure that only they receive and decrypt the video keys. The video's symmetric keys are shared that way to ensure that even the server doesn't have access to the key.

How do I know that the receiver won't share the video he receives?

Our proprietary technology ensures that the intended user can't record their screen when viewing the video. And even if they use their phone to record, the watermark with their personal information will guarantee that you know who is sharing your content
